Pink Flamingo Challenge

Then extend the program to say “Flamingo” when a number is a member of the Fibonacci sequence, and “Pink Flamingo” when it is a multiple of 3 and 5 and a member of the Fibonacci sequence. Hint: A number is Fibonacci if and only if one or both of 5 * n or is a perfect square 2 + 4 5 * n 2 − 4 (i.e. A number made by squaring a whole number).
